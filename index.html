Biography

The Journey of Data Discovery

I am Harsh Yadav, a graduate in Information Technology and Analytics from Rutgers Business School, where I built strong expertise in statistics, mathematics, database management, and machine learning methods. My master’s program sharpened my skills in data wrangling, predictive modeling, visualization, and business analytics, preparing me to solve complex, data-driven challenges.

During my Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics at Mumbai University, I studied calculus, linear algebra, probability, statistics, and optimization techniques. This coursework strengthened my analytical foundation, enhanced my quantitative problem-solving skills, and provided exposure to statistical modeling, data-driven decision-making, and pattern recognition.

My analytical process begins with data cleaning and preprocessing, followed by exploratory data analysis (EDA) to uncover patterns, trends, and anomalies. I then apply statistical models, machine learning algorithms, and formula-driven approaches to solve business problems, presenting results as actionable insights through Tableau, Power BI, Excel, and Python (Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly).

Currently working as an Analyst at Alvy Dental in Iselin, NJ. I focus on growth through collaboration and continuous learning, consistently upskilling through new projects, peer feedback, and industry research to deliver measurable business impact.
